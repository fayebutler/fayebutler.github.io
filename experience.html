Wonder Woman Project

See the site here.

I worked with producers and designers to create a progressive web app to be used at the Wonder Woman premieres across many territories. The app allowed you to take photos, add Wonder Woman stickers, save and share your final photo. The photos are saved to an s3 bucket for moderation then pulled from the approved list into a gallery on the site. This was mostly written in php with gulp automation, I also had to make sure it was fully templated as it then had to be localised into multiple languages.

The Good Doctor Project

After being granted access to Facebook's beta AR Studio I quickly had to learn how to use it and create an AR filter that included multiple elements and triggers for ABC. This was a particularly difficult project as we had many iterations and feedback and communication had to be planned well as we were working with a client in the US.

We also created a filter for TGIT and they were featured together on adweek.
